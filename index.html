Welcome to the Roulette Simulator!

The Rules are:

Select on what you want to bet on. For example, if you want to bet on all of the red numbers, select 'red' in the betting section. The same applies for black, even, and odd numbers. If you wish to bet on a specific number, then chose the 'specific' option.Wagers work as follows: Bets on Red, Black, Odds, or Evens pay out 2 to 1 (double your money). Bets on Specific numbers pay out 10 to 1 (10 times your money). You can only place 1 large bet per game (bets on Odds, Evens, Blacks, and Reds).

How to Play:

Click 'Add Funds' to add money to your account, click 'Play' to start placing bets, click 'Spin the Wheel' to spin the wheel, and press 'Check my Bets' to see if you won!
